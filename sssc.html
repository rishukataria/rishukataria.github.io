Welcome To Our Shop

Shree Shanthi Sales Corp was started in the year 1981 as an sub-branch of our head office . The ultimate goal of opening the shop was to enhance the needs of our customers and add more varieties of fabric into it. We deal with various brands like Mafatlal , Birla Century, Coimbatore Processing Mill , Parag Rubia ,Arvinde Sajjani etc..
We keep full Stock in CPM Archna, Parag Rubia, Fancy Blouse , CPM Chandrakanta , Sara Queen Delux , Sanjay Madhvi , Arvinde Sajjani Linning, Fancy Sarees, School Uniforms , Shirting and Sutting and other materials.

At Shree Shanthi sales Corp we deal with high quality fabrics and always ensure that our customers get the best out of us. We also promise to deliver 100% customer statisfaction all the time.
